About Us

Divyash and Sahil: Co-founders

This project was inspired for the 2020 Congressional App Challenge. Due to COVID-19, many people are stuck at home and can't experience life as they should. Many others don't have access to fancy VR equipment to pretend to go outdoors and thus SOLO-VR was formed.


Solo-VR aims to help everyone in their reach to experience the world. From attending religious services to going to the park, SOLO-VR allows that to come from the safety of your home
